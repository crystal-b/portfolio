Layout: Book Covers

Group Members: Ashley Annis, Megan Barcia, Kaylie Belcik, Alexis Billinge, Jessica Cabrera

For my Issues in Publishing class, my group created a catalog of imaginary new releases for our fictional publishing company. Each member was the editor of a specific genre for young adult readers.

My contribution: I designed book covers for 15 of the 18 publications to legitimize our sales presentation. I collaborated with one of the editors who had very detailed ideas on what images and color schemes to use for her books, which included Don’t Mind the Intern and Social Butterfly. For the remaining titles, the other editors allowed me free rein.

I tried to incorporate elements of the plot in the cover design. For example, Amulet of Illusion involves a choker with a velvet strap, so I placed the author’s name in a strip to simulate the choker and chose a background that calls to mind red velvet. Because the story is a mystery, I also repeatedly layered the title to illustrate the varying accounts.
