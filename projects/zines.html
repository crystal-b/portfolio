Layout: Zines

After a movie I was eager to see released a terrible poster, I spent a chilly fall weekend creating Hulking Beetle. This zine highlights movie posters made by talented designers.

For some of the posters, I had to do quite a bit of research to find the artist or agency responsible. I even called and emailed a few distributors to get some more background on the designs. Hulking Beetle is a small, pithy zine with some real weight behind it.

Steven Wells was a writer whose work appeared in The Guardian, CMJ, and Philadelphia Weekly, among others. After Wells passed, I saved as many of his pieces that I could find. The organizations he wrote for eventually removed his articles from their websites, so I created a physical version of his work.

Wells used intense imagery in much of his writing, so I tried to build a humorous dialog between the design and the articles.
