Prototype: IXDA '13 Challenge

The Interaction Design Association 2013 challenge was to redesign the child health record. I participated as a challenge to myself, but did not actually submit my idea. Part of the focus was to improve usability for illiterate parents, so I incorporated audio feedback and iconography to help aid understanding.
