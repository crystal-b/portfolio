Layout: Zines

After a movie I was eager to see released a terrible poster, I spent a chilly fall weekend creating this zine. It highlights movie posters made by talented designers. For some of the posters, I had to do quite a bit of research to find the artist or agency responsible. I even called and emailed a few distributors to get some more background on the designs. Hulking Beetle is a small, pithy zine with some real weight behind it.

Prototype: Watches + Clocks

This project involved planning a redesign of a local business website for my Information Design class. The original website had a confusing structure including two different homepages. As part of the planning process, I cataloged all of the current pages, determined which ones were unnecessary, added and consolidated pages as needed, diagrammed workflows of engaging interactive elements like allowing users to explore watchmakers on a world map.

Prototype: Goal Tracker

For my Designing Meaningful Interactions class, I had to create a physical object that allowed users to count up or down. The class received two user scenarios. One was for Rachel, who wiles away the days at work waiting for her next vacation. The other was for Jon, who is trying to cut back on his daily coffee consumption in order to sleep better. While both Rachel and Jon were essentially counting, I thought of this project more like goal tracking.
